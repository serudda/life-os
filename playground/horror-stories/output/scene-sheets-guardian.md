## Scene 1: The Accident

### Original Text

> Morí hace ocho años.
>
> No fue particularmente trágico. Ni inusual. Solo un accidente de auto. No culpo al hombre que me atropelló. Iba con exceso de velocidad porque su esposa estaba en trabajo de parto, y había hielo negro en la carretera. Perdió el control del coche y yo perdí la vida.
>
> No fue su culpa. Lo sé. No soy cruel. No soy vengativo.
>
> Si acaso, soy lo contrario.

### Characters Present

- **GHOST — The Guardian**: Figura en el suelo o siendo impactada por el vehículo. Aún humano en este momento (pre-fantasma), pero puede representarse ya como la silueta amorf para mantener consistencia visual. Wearing: la misma ropa dañada que llevará como fantasma — chaqueta y pantalón, pre-destrucción o en el momento del impacto.
- **JACK — Father**: Dentro del vehículo o implícito por los faros del auto. No necesita ser visible directamente — el auto lo representa. Wearing: no visible.

### Setting

- **Location**: Carretera suburbana en invierno
- **Time of day**: Noche
- **Weather/atmosphere**: Hielo negro en la carretera. Aire helado. Vapor visible. Oscuridad total excepto por los faros del auto.

### Key Action

Un cuerpo yace en una carretera congelada mientras los faros de un auto fuera de control iluminan la escena a través del vapor nocturno.

### Visual Elements

- Objects: Auto descontrolado (o solo sus faros como fuente de luz), hielo negro en el asfalto, cuerpo en el suelo
- Background details: Carretera vacía extendiéndose en ambas direcciones, árboles desnudos de invierno flanqueando, oscuridad total más allá de la luz

### Camera & Composition

- **Shot type**: Wide shot — plano general cinemático
- **Focus**: Los faros cortando la oscuridad e iluminando la figura en el suelo
- **Character positioning**: La figura (GHOST pre-muerte) en el centro-bajo del cuadro. El auto o sus faros en el tercio izquierdo o derecho, creando una diagonal de luz. Perspectiva de ángulo bajo desde el nivel del asfalto.

### Emotional Tone

Fatalidad serena

### Whisk Prompt (EN)

**Subject**: Ghost figure lying on icy ground, damaged clothes, motionless
**Scene**: Frozen night road, headlights cutting through darkness, black ice, bare winter trees
**Prompt refinement**: Body on frozen road illuminated by headlights through steam and darkness

## Scene 2: The Funeral

### Original Text

> Verás, ya no tenía familia y había perdido a mis pocos amigos por esa época. Cuando llegó el momento de mi funeral, las únicas personas que asistieron fueron mi jefe y la familia que me mató. La esposa sostenía a su hija recién nacida contra su pecho.
>
> Odiaba a mi jefe, y el cementerio era terriblemente solitario, así que seguí a la familia hasta su casa.

### Characters Present

- **GHOST — The Guardian**: Observando desde la distancia, parcialmente fundido con una lápida o un árbol del cementerio. Silueta amorf difusa en el fondo. Wearing: chaqueta y pantalón dañados.
- **JACK — Father**: De pie junto a la tumba, postura rígida de culpa y respeto. Wearing: abrigo oscuro sobre su ropa habitual.
- **LORI — Mother**: Sosteniendo a Lily recién nacida contra su pecho con ambos brazos. Wearing: abrigo largo, bufanda.
- **LILY — Daughter (recién nacida)**: Envuelta en mantas contra el pecho de Lori. Solo se ve un bulto pequeño y claro.
- **BOSS — Former boss**: De pie apartado del grupo familiar. Figura oscura genérica de traje. Wearing: traje oscuro.

### Setting

- **Location**: Cementerio suburbano
- **Time of day**: Día gris, luz difusa — sin sol directo
- **Weather/atmosphere**: Frío. Cielo opresivo y pesado. Sensación de vacío absoluto — el cementerio está casi desierto.

### Key Action

Cuatro personas rodean una tumba en un cementerio desolado mientras una quinta figura — amorf y oscura — observa desde las sombras, decidiendo seguirlos.

### Visual Elements

- Objects: Tumba abierta o lápida fresca, flores mínimas, bebé envuelta en mantas
- Background details: Lápidas dispersas extendiéndose hasta el horizonte, árboles sin hojas, cielo pesado con cross-hatching denso, vacío opresivo

### Camera & Composition

- **Shot type**: Wide shot — plano general
- **Focus**: El pequeño grupo alrededor de la tumba, con el Ghost como una presencia periférica que el ojo descubre después
- **Character positioning**: Jack y Lori (con Lily) juntos en el centro, cerca de la tumba. El Boss a 2-3 metros de distancia, separado. El Ghost en el borde del cuadro — detrás de un árbol o lápida, parcialmente fundido con el entorno. Perspectiva ligeramente elevada mirando hacia abajo.

### Emotional Tone

Soledad desoladora

### Whisk Prompt (EN)

**Subject**: Small group at a grave, woman holding newborn, dark figure watching from distance
**Scene**: Desolate cemetery, bare trees, heavy overcast sky, scattered headstones, oppressive emptiness
**Prompt refinement**: Lonely funeral in empty cemetery, shadowy figure watching from behind headstones

## Scene 3: Rocking the Cradle

### Original Text

> Lily bien podría haber sido de mi propia carne y sangre. Era dulce, brillante y tan, tan pequeña. Tenía problemas para dormir si alguien no mecía su cuna y sus padres estaban tan cansados. Después de que la acostaban, era fácil para mí mecer su cuna por ella. Yo no me cansaba. Podía ayudarla.

### Characters Present

- **GHOST — The Guardian**: Standing next to the cradle, a defined hand resting on the edge rocking it. The rest of the body blends with the darkness of the room. Leaning, protective posture. Wearing: damaged jacket and pants, barely visible — dominated by darkness.
- **LILY — Daughter (baby)**: Inside the cradle, a small light form wrapped in blankets. Wearing: baby clothes / blankets.

### Setting

- **Location**: Baby's room in the family home
- **Time of day**: Night — early morning
- **Weather/atmosphere**: Silence. Intimate darkness broken only by moonlight or a nightlight. Absolute calm.

### Key Action

A defined hand emerging from a dark, amorphous mass gently rocks a cradle where a tiny baby sleeps, bathed in moonlight.

### Visual Elements

- Objects: Wooden cradle, blankets, nightlight or window with moonlight, possibly a hanging mobile over the cradle
- Background details: Baby's room in twilight, walls barely visible, the window as the only light source creating a volumetric beam

### Camera & Composition

- **Shot type**: Medium shot — with a slight high angle
- **Focus**: The Ghost's hand on the edge of the cradle — the point of contact between the dark entity and the world of the living
- **Character positioning**: The cradle in the center of the frame. The Ghost on the right, leaning over it — its dark mass occupies the right and upper third. Lily (baby) barely visible inside the cradle as a small, light form. Moonlight entering from the left, creating a diagonal of light that illuminates the cradle but leaves the Ghost in shadows. Slightly elevated perspective.

### Emotional Tone

Supernatural tenderness

### Whisk Prompt (EN)

**Subject**: Dark amorphous figure with defined hand gently rocking a baby cradle
**Scene**: Dark nursery at night, moonlight through window, volumetric light beam, wooden cradle
**Prompt refinement**: Ghost hand gently rocking cradle in moonlit nursery, tender darkness

## Scene 4: Part of the Family

### Original Text

> Con el paso de los años, Jack y Lori se dieron cuenta de que no estaban solos en la casa. No tardaron mucho en conectar mi funeral con el momento en que aparecí. Y como nunca fui malintencionado, no tenían miedo ni estaban enojados.
>
> Empezaron a encender velas en el aniversario del día de mi muerte. Dejaban una silla vacía en las comidas y en las festividades. Realmente sentí que… era un miembro de la familia.

### Characters Present

- **GHOST — The Guardian**: Not fully visible — its silhouette is hinted at in or around the empty chair. Like a density in the air, a subtle darkening. Present but respectfully semi-materialized. Wearing: barely suggested.
- **JACK — Father**: Sitting at the table, relaxed. Serene expression — without fear. Wearing: flannel shirt, jeans.
- **LORI — Mother**: Sitting next to Jack, looking toward the empty chair with a warm expression. Wearing: oversized cardigan.
- **LILY — Daughter (~5-6 years)**: Sitting at the table, legs dangling from the chair. Natural, cheerful expression. Wearing: simple dress.

### Setting

- **Location**: Dining room of the family home
- **Time of day**: Night — dinner
- **Weather/atmosphere**: Warm interior. The brightest scene in the entire story. Candles dominate the lighting, creating a warm chiaroscuro instead of the usual cold.

### Key Action

A family dines together around a table with an empty chair and a lit candle, while the ghost's silhouette is faintly hinted at in the empty space — accepted, loved, present.

### Visual Elements

- Objects: Dining table with plates, an empty chair, lit candle(s), food, possibly small offerings (trinkets) near the candle
- Background details: Warm domestic interior, blurred kitchen or living room in the background, pictures or shelves barely visible on the walls

### Camera & Composition

- **Shot type**: Medium shot — from one end of the table
- **Focus**: The empty chair with the candle — the point where the supernatural and the domestic meet
- **Character positioning**: Jack and Lori on one side of the table. Lily next to one of them. The empty chair on the opposite side, facing the viewer or at a three-quarter angle. The Ghost hinted as a subtle density in/on the chair — not a defined figure, more as if the air were darker there. The candle illuminates the empty chair, creating the warmest chiaroscuro of the entire series.

### Emotional Tone

Warm belonging

### Whisk Prompt (EN)

**Subject**: Family of three at dinner table, empty chair with lit candle, faint shadow presence
**Scene**: Warm domestic dining room, candlelight chiaroscuro, cozy interior, plates on table
**Prompt refinement**: Family dinner with empty chair and candle, faint ghostly presence in warmth

## Scene 5: The Break-In

### Original Text

> Alguien está intentando forzar la puerta.
>
> Es el ex de Lori. Está obsesionado. Está furioso. Va a hacerle daño a la familia. A mi familia.

### Characters Present

- **EX — Antagonist**: His angular silhouette visible through a side window or the crack of the door being forced. Aggressive posture, leaning against the door. Wearing: dark tight hoodie.
- **LORI — Mother**: Back in the hallway, standing protectively over Lily. Expression of controlled terror. Wearing: cardigan.
- **LILY — Daughter (~8 years old)**: Clinging to Lori, partially hidden behind her. Wearing: pajamas or simple dress.

### Setting

- **Location**: Entrance of the family home, seen from the interior
- **Time of day**: Night
- **Weather/atmosphere**: Maximum tension. Exterior darkness penetrating. Implicit sound of creaking wood and metal being forced.

### Key Action

The front door deforms under the force of an angular and menacing silhouette while at the end of the hallway a mother embraces her daughter.

### Visual Elements

- Objects: Main door deforming or opening, forced lock, side window glass, house hallway
- Background details: Domestic hallway stretching back with expressionist forced perspective, exterior darkness visible through the door edges, angular and distorted shadows

### Camera & Composition

- **Shot type**: Wide shot with expressionist forced perspective
- **Focus**: The door deforming — the point of rupture between safety and threat
- **Character positioning**: The door dominates the foreground, occupying the left third of the frame, with the Ex's silhouette visible through the glass or the crack. The hallway stretches toward the right/background with exaggerated perspective. Lori and Lily at the end of the hallway, small due to perspective — emphasizing their vulnerability. The door is massive, the family is small. No Ghost presence in this scene — its absence is intentional, building tension.

### Emotional Tone

Imminent threat

### Whisk Prompt (EN)

**Subject**: Angular menacing silhouette forcing a door, mother and child huddled in background
**Scene**: House entrance at night, door bulging inward, expressionist hallway stretching deep, heavy shadows
**Prompt refinement**: Violent silhouette breaking door while mother shields child in dark hallway

## Scene 6: The Knife

### Original Text

> La cuestión con los fantasmas es que, cuantas más ofrendas recibes, más fuerte te vuelves. Había estado disfrutando de velas, baratijas e incluso algún que otro alimento durante los últimos cinco años. Gracias a eso, era fuerte.
>
> El cuchillo se siente cálido en mi mano. Una descarga de calor contra el hielo de mi piel.
>
> Lori, Jack y Lily son mi familia. Me importan. Y no se van a unir a mí todavía.

### Characters Present

- **GHOST — The Guardian**: Fully manifested. Larger than in previous scenes — empowered by years of offerings. Dominant amorphous silhouette, standing between the threat and the family. A defined hand gripping a kitchen knife that catches all the light in the scene. Wearing: damaged jacket and pants, more visible than ever.
- **JACK — Father**: Behind the Ghost, protecting Lori and Lily. Wearing: flannel shirt.
- **LORI — Mother**: Behind the Ghost, next to Jack, holding Lily. Wearing: cardigan.
- **LILY — Daughter (~8 years old)**: Next to her parents, behind the Ghost. Wearing: pajamas.

### Setting

- **Location**: Interior of the family home — living room or hallway near the entrance
- **Time of day**: Night
- **Weather/atmosphere**: Raw energy. The air feels dense. Total darkness except for the glow of the knife and the edges of the Ghost which now emit a faint luminosity — the power of the offerings made visible.

### Key Action

The ghost stands tall wielding a glowing knife between the intruder and its adoptive family — an entity of pure protective darkness with the brightest weapon in the entire story.

### Visual Elements

- Objects: Kitchen knife (the brightest point in the entire series), visible offerings in the background (extinguished candles, trinkets on a shelf), forced door in the background
- Background details: Domestic interior now turned into a battlefield, furniture barely visible, the broken door in the background letting in the exterior darkness

### Camera & Composition

- **Shot type**: Wide shot with low angle — worm's eye view
- **Focus**: The knife in the Ghost's hand — the brightest point and the center of the entire composition. The eye goes from the knife to the Ghost's silhouette, and then to the family behind.
- **Character positioning**: The Ghost dominates the center of the frame, seen from below (low angle), occupying most of the vertical space. Its silhouette is the largest it has been in the entire series. The hand with the knife extended forward — toward where the intruder is (off-camera or at the edge). The family (Jack, Lori, Lily) grouped behind the Ghost in the lower right third, illuminated by the reflection of the knife. The Ghost is shield and shadow simultaneously. Triangular composition: knife (top) → Ghost (center) → family (bottom-back).

### Emotional Tone

Protective determination

### Whisk Prompt (EN)

**Subject**: Massive dark amorphous figure gripping a glowing knife, family huddled behind
**Scene**: Dark house interior, low angle, broken door in background, dramatic chiaroscuro
**Prompt refinement**: Ghost wielding bright knife protecting family, low angle, powerful darkness
