## Raw Content

I keep thinking of this term in my head because it's so good "Shotgun to Sniper Strategy" @marclou coined it

You want to start with a shotgun strategy where you try lots of ideas, launch lots of projects, MVPs etc. add a Stripe buy button to everything

Then see what really gets traction, goes viral, makes money (!) and then what keeps making money over time (like months)

Then you wanna sniper and focus on that project to make it big

I love the shotgun to sniper analogy So good

## Key Points

- Start with "shotgun" approach: try many ideas, launch multiple MVPs
- Add monetization (Stripe) to everything from the start
- Observe what gets traction, goes viral, and makes money
- Wait to see what keeps making money over time (months)
- Then switch to "sniper" mode: focus intensely on the winner
